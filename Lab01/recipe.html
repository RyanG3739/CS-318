Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tb minced garlic
  • 1/ teaspoon red pepper flake
  • 3 skinless, bonless chicken breast
  • 2 tb sesame oil
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 1 green bell pepper

Instructions

  • Step 1: Bring water to boil, reduce heat and simmer rice
  • Step 2: combine sauce, brown sugar, stir till smooth. Stir in red pepper flakes, add chicken.
  • Step 3: Heat sesame oil in skillet, add broccoli, onionn, carrots, pepper.
  • Step 4: Heat sesame oil and add chicken marinade, cook until browned or about 5-7 miunutes.

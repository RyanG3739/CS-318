My Favorite Pizza

My favorite pizza is a chicken bacon ranch pizza. It is made by taking a pizza crust and adding ranch as the sauce and then putting mozzerela cheese, chicken chunks, and bacon on it. Sometimes I will put artichokes on it as well. My favorite place to get this pizza is from Domino's.
